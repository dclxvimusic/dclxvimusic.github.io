DCLXVI is a producer from Tashkent, Uzbekistan known for blending atmospheric phonk, wave-inspired textures, and cinematic electronic production. His sound combines dark immersive melodies, distorted rhythms, and emotional depth, creating a distinct sonic identity shaped by atmosphere and mood.

Influenced by underground hip-hop culture, ambient electronic music, and cinematic sound design, DCLXVI focuses on immersive production that balances heavy energy with melancholic textures. Releases such as "LOST", "DISSECTION", and "ERROR" helped define the darker direction of his sound and introduced his music to a growing international audience.

With listeners across the United States, Europe, and beyond, DCLXVI continues to evolve his artistic vision through atmospheric and emotionally driven production.
